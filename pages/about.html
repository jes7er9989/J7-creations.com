About J7 Technical Solutions

Professional IT infrastructure and custom fabrication services for West Tennessee.

About

I'm Thomas Ipock. I run J7 Technical Solutions & Prototyping out of Milan/Atwood, TN.

I provide integrated IT infrastructure, custom fabrication, and field installation services. My work combines network design, custom parts fabrication, and professional on-site deployment — the kind of projects that need more than one specialty.

My background includes roles at Tesla, SWITCH, Lutron Electronics, and Geek Squad, spanning high-voltage electrical systems, enterprise IT networks, hardware/software support, and custom manufacturing.

Service Approach

I handle projects that don't have off-the-shelf solutions. When standard equipment doesn't fit, when network coverage gaps persist, or when custom mounting is required — I design and deploy integrated solutions.

Our work combines three core capabilities:

  • IT Infrastructure — Network design, VLAN segmentation, PoE deployment, Home Assistant automation
  • Custom Fabrication — 3D printing, CAD design, laser etching, replacement parts, enclosures
  • Field Installation — Equipment mounting, cable management, testing, commissioning

Operating Principles

  • No black boxes — Every system I build is documented, explainable, and under your control. No hidden dependencies, no cloud lock-in, no mystery.
  • Frontloaded planning — I invest time in design upfront to prevent problems later.
  • Local deployment — Systems operate on-site without cloud dependencies or subscriptions.
  • Professional execution — Clean installations, proper documentation, post-deployment support.

Service Area

Based in Milan/Atwood, TN, I serve a 100-mile radius covering Jackson, Martin, Union City, Dyersburg, Paris, Huntingdon, and surrounding communities.
