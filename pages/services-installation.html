What's Included

Professional Mounting

I don't just screw things to the wall. I find studs, use proper anchors, level everything, and ensure a secure, long-lasting install.

Cable Management

Hidden cables where possible, bundled and dressed where visible. I treat your space with respect — no dangling wires, no drill dust left behind.

Testing & Commissioning

I don't leave until everything works. Network tested, cameras angled and recording, automations triggered, client trained.

Cleanup

I clean up after myself. Vacuum drill dust, remove packaging, leave your space as I found it (but with working tech).
