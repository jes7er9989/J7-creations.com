What I Do

Whole-Property WiFi

No more dead zones. I design and deploy enterprise-grade networks that cover your entire property — house, barn, shop, yard.

VLAN Segmentation

Keep your IoT devices, guests, and main network separate. Security by design — your smart TV can't talk to your laptop.

Perfect for: Security-conscious users, smart homes, small businesses.

Home Assistant Automation

Smart home features without the cloud. Lights, cameras, sensors — all local, no subscriptions, works even when internet is down.

PoE++ Deployment

Self-Hosted AI

Perfect for: Privacy-focused AI, local processing, data sovereignty.

Perfect for: Troubleshooting, security reviews, performance optimization.
