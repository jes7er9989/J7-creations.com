What I Print

Replacement Parts

Automotive clips, appliance latches, machinery guides, HVAC components — if it's plastic and they don't make it anymore, we'll print it better than the original.

Common jobs: Car door clips, dishwasher racks, conveyor guides, vent covers.

Custom Mounts & Brackets

Network gear, cameras, sensors, tools, automotive equipment — I design and print mounts that fit your space, not off-the-shelf compromises.

Common jobs: AP ceiling mounts, camera brackets, tool holders, battery racks.

Enclosures & Housings

Weatherproof boxes, control panel enclosures, protective covers — custom sizes, gasket channels, mounting points.

Common jobs: Sensor housings, electrical boxes, equipment covers, outdoor enclosures.

Signage & Displays

Lighted signs, nameplates, business displays — designed, printed, and wired. Ready to hang.

Common jobs: Business signs, warning labels, laser-etched nameplates, display stands.

Prototypes

Got an idea? We'll print it fast so you can test fit, function, and form before committing to production.

Common jobs: Product prototypes, concept models, fitment testing, iterative design.

Laser Etching

Permanent serialization, branding, warning labels on printed parts. Traceability built in.

Common jobs: Serial numbers, logos, QR codes, safety labels, part numbers.

Materials I Print

PLA

Best for: Prototypes, display models, test fits

Properties: Easy to print, biodegradable, low heat resistance

Temp Range: Up to 50°C (122°F)

Colors: Full range available

PETG

Best for: Functional parts, outdoor use, chemical exposure

Properties: Strong, flexible, chemical resistant

Temp Range: Up to 80°C (176°F)

Colors: Clear, black, white, colors

ABS

Best for: High-impact applications, automotive

Properties: Tough, durable, can be smoothed/finished

Temp Range: Up to 100°C (212°F)

Colors: Black, white, gray

ASA

Best for: Outdoor parts, UV exposure, automotive

Properties: UV stable, weather resistant, strong

Temp Range: Up to 100°C (212°F)

Colors: Black, white, gray

TPU

Best for: Flexible parts, gaskets, bumpers, grips

Properties: Rubber-like, flexible, abrasion resistant

Temp Range: Up to 80°C (176°F)

Hardness: 95A (flexible)

PC (Polycarbonate)

Best for: Extreme strength, high-temp applications

Properties: Very strong, heat resistant, transparent

Temp Range: Up to 145°C (293°F)

Colors: Clear, black

Nylon PA6

Best for: Wear-resistant parts, gears, bearings

Properties: Tough, low friction, chemical resistant

Temp Range: Up to 180°C (356°F)

Colors: Natural, black

Nylon PA12

Best for: Functional prototypes, end-use parts

Properties: Excellent detail, strong, flexible

Temp Range: Up to 175°C (347°F)

Colors: Natural, black, gray

Carbon Fiber (CF)

Best for: High-strength, lightweight structural parts

Properties: Extremely stiff, strong, lightweight

Temp Range: Varies by base material (typically 100-180°C)

Colors: Black (carbon weave visible)

Glass Fiber (GF)

Best for: Strong, rigid parts at lower cost than CF

Properties: High strength, stiff, good heat resistance

Temp Range: Varies by base material (typically 100-160°C)

Colors: Natural, black, gray

How 3D Printing Pricing Works

Two-part pricing: Filament + Print Service Fee

  • Filament cost: Priced based on Bambu Lab retail pricing. Covers material, shipping, and storage.
  • Print service fee: Covers machine time, power, wear, setup/slicing, and basic post-processing (support removal, cleaning).
  • Total cost: Filament + Print Fee = Your Price. Detailed quote provided before printing.

Design work, laser etching, and advanced post-processing (sanding, vapor smoothing, painting) are quoted separately.
